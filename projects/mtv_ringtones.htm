*MTV is MTV's branded content delivered on Virgin Mobile's cellular service network. The Ringtone Store allows subscribers to browse for polyphonic and monophonic ringtones of songs by their favorite artists. Subscribers can select ringtones through three access points: browsing on the web by artist, category, and favorites; through a series of WAP menus on the phone; or through a IVR voice application that searches the catalog via voice recognition.The application was built using a combination of VXML, WML, and served using the Dynamo Application Server.
