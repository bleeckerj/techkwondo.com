*MTV is MTV's branded content delivered on Virgin Mobile's cellular service network. This application allows subscribers to browse through, using their *MTV phones, notable audio clips drawn from popular on-air shows such as The Osbournes and Real World and send these to their friends in the form of an "audio postcard" complete with a recorded audio message. The application was built using a combination of VXML, SMS, and served using the Dynamo Application Server.
